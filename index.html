About Shalom Radio

Connecting Kabala to the world through quality broadcasting

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Shalom Radio has grown to become Kabala's most trusted source of news, entertainment, and community information. What began as a small local station has expanded its reach to serve Sierra Leoneans worldwide.

Our mission is to inform, educate, and entertain while preserving our cultural heritage and promoting community development. We take pride in being the voice of Kabala, bringing people together through the power of radio.

Our Values

  • Integrity in all our reporting
  • Commitment to community service
  • Cultural preservation and promotion
  • Innovation in broadcasting
  • Accessibility for all listeners

Our Reach

With our FM signal covering all of Kabala and surrounding districts, plus our online streaming platform and mobile app, we serve thousands of listeners daily across Sierra Leone and the global diaspora.
